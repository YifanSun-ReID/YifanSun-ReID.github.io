I am currently a Senior Expert at Baidu Research (since 2021). Before joining Baidu, I was a senior researcher at MegVii Inc. and an optical remote sensing institute, respectively. I received the B.E. degree, M.S. (Optical Engineering) degree and Ph.D. (Electronics Engineering) degree from Tsinghua University in 2005, 2008 and 2019. My research interests include person re-identification, deep metric learning, long-tailed visual recognition, domain adaptation and generalization.
